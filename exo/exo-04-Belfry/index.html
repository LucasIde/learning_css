Welcom to the belfry whitby

Once you walk through our front door, everything feels like home. Here you’ll sleep soundly and dine in style, always with our great value and friendly service…

Providing contemporary bed and breakfast accommodation in a stunning Victorian home stylishly renovated to the highest standards. Located in the grand West Cliff area of Whitby we’re just a leisurely stroll from the long sandy beach, town centre and the beautiful historic harbour, where you can appreciate the delights of the many restaurants, cafes and bars Whitby has to offer.

We pride ourselves on our friendly relaxed atmosphere with us, your hosts Phill & Sophie, on hand whenever you need us. Our Yorkshire Breakfast is cooked freshly to order using the best local ingredients on offer.

Unwind with your family and friends in our guest lounge stocked with lots of useful local information, a guest fridge and bean to cup coffee machine.

A breakfast to return for

A leisurely affair, breakfast is served between 8:00 and 9:30, allowing you plenty of time make the most of the day ahead or stay in bed that little bit longer. Offered each day are fresh fruit, cereals, yoghurt, and fruit juices; followed by a choice of a number of cooked options including our traditional Yorkshire breakfast or one of Sophie’s daily specials.
